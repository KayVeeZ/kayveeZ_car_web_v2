Web development is a vital facet of the tech industry, revolutionizing how we interact with information online. From front-end design to back-end functionality, it encompasses a range of technologies and practices. HTML, CSS, and JavaScript form the foundation, shaping the user interface and experience. Frameworks like React and Angular streamline development, enhancing interactivity and responsiveness. Back-end technologies such as Node.js or Django manage server-side logic and database interactions. As the digital landscape evolves, web development remains a key driver of innovation, adapting to meet the changing needs of users and businesses.
